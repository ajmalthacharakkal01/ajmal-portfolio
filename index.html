About Me

I'm a passionate electronics and robotics enthusiast currently working as a robotics intern at AISOTOP, which is part of BAIRUHATECH. With a background in Applied Electronics and Instrumentation, I'm exploring the exciting world of robotics and automation while also taking my first steps into web development.

Since November 2024, I have been working as an Electronics Project Trainer at PROJECTX, guiding students to explore the fundamentals of electronics and embedded systems. This experience has not only deepened my technical expertise but also sharpened my ability to explain complex ideas in a simple and engaging way.

I'm currently learning web development, starting with HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript. While I'm a beginner in this field, I'm excited to combine my electronics background with web technologies. I believe this combination will help me create better user interfaces for robotics projects and develop more comprehensive technical solutions.

When I'm not working on robotics or electronics projects, you can find me practicing web development, watching coding tutorials, or experimenting with new technologies. I'm always eager to learn and appreciate the journey of growing from a beginner to a more experienced developer.
